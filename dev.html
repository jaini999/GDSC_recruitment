Understanding Graphs and Their Importance

Graphs are essential tools in data visualization, helping us represent information in a visual and simplified manner. Different types of graphs serve different purposes, whether you want to showcase trends, compare values, or track growth.

Bar Graph

A bar graph displays data using rectangular bars. Each bar represents a category of data, and the length of the bar is proportional to the value of the data.

Line Graph

A line graph shows information as a series of data points called 'markers' connected by straight line segments. It is often used to visualize a trend in data over intervals of time.

Pie Chart

A pie chart is a circular statistical graphic, divided into slices to illustrate numerical proportions. It helps to visualize part-to-whole relationships in data.
